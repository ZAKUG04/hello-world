Sports Nutrition for Performance & Muscle Growth

Why Nutrition Matters

Sports nutrition is a key factor in athletic performance. According to sports science research, proper intake of carbohydrates, proteins, and fats directly affects muscle growth, recovery, and training performance.

Macronutrients in Training

Carbohydrates

The main energy source for training. They replenish glycogen, which fuels strength and endurance workouts.

Proteins

Essential for muscle repair and growth. Helps rebuild muscle fibers after resistance training.

Fats

Support hormone production and long-term energy. Important for overall health and recovery.

Body Types & Nutrition Strategy

Ectomorph: Needs higher carbs and calories to gain muscle mass.

Mesomorph: Balanced diet with moderate carbs and high protein.

Endomorph: Lower carbs, higher protein and healthy fats for fat control.
