Essential Gym Machines Guide

How Gym Machines Work

Gym machines are designed to guide your movement path, reduce injury risk, and isolate specific muscles for better training efficiency.

Main Training Machines

Chest Press

Simulates a bench press movement. Works chest, shoulders, and triceps by pushing weight forward in a controlled path.

Leg Press

Targets lower body muscles like quads, glutes, and hamstrings. You push a weighted platform using your legs.

Lat Pulldown

Strengthens the back and biceps. You pull a bar downward against resistance to build upper body strength.

Cable Machine

A versatile machine used for multiple exercises. Allows constant tension for muscles during movement.
